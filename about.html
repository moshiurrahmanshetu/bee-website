Our Story

About Honey & Hive

Born from a love of nature and a deep respect for the bees that sustain our world.

The Beginning

How It All Started

It began with a single hive in the Sundarban forest. Our founder, captivated by the intricate world of bees, spent years learning the ancient art of ethical beekeeping from local communities.

What started as a passion project soon grew into a mission — to bring the purest, most authentic honey directly from nature's heart to your table, without any compromise on quality or ethics.
