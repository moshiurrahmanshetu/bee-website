The Harvest Process

  • 1. Foraging: Bees collect nectar from local organic blooms.
  • 2. Maturation: Nectar is converted and sealed in wax combs.
  • 3. Extraction: We carefully spin frames leaving wax intact.
  • 4. Bottling: Directly poured without micro-filtering.
